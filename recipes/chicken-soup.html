Chicken Soup

Description

Ingredients

  • One whole chicken, ~1.5kgs
  • Four carrots
  • Four celery sticks
  • One large onion
  • One chicken stock cube

Steps

  1. Remove the onion skin and halve the veg.

  2. Boil the chicken and veg until the meat is falling off the
    bone.

  3. Seive out the water into another pot.

  4. Add chicken stock cube to the water and keep on low heat.

  5. Chop the veg and shred the chicken. Discard the bones and skin.

  6. Add everything back into the pot. Heat through and season to
    taste.
