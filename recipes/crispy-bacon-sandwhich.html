Crispy Bacon Sandwhich

Description

The perfect meal for a lazy Sunday morning, or for an upset tummy.

Ingredients

  • Four full size rashers of thin cut bacon
  • Lurpak butter
  • Thick cut white bread

Steps

  1. Melt a generous tablespoon of butter on high heat. Wait until it is fully melted (but well before it browns).

    • (You may want to put your tub of butter back into the fridge at this point - it is important it is not half melted by the time you butter your sandwhich!).

  2. Begin adding the bacon rashers. If the pan is smaller, you may not have room for all four, so you can stagger them.

  3. Heat until fat is sizzling at the top of each rasher and it has shrunk by a third to a half (~ 2-3 mins).

  4. Flip the bacon and heat for another ~2 mins until the rashers have shrunk by another third or so.

  5. Flip a final time and ensure both sides have the classic "caramelised" brown edges and ridges before turning off the heat.

  6. Place the rashers onto some kitchen roll to get rid of the excess fat, and meanwhile butter your bread generously.

  7. Add the bacon to the sandwhich, and voila, you have the perfect crispy bacon sandwhich!
